# Text

## Header

### Basic
<pre>
# h1
## h2
### h3
#### h4
##### h5
</pre>

<pre>
h1
============
</pre>

<pre>
h2
-----------
</pre>

### ID's
<pre>
### Hello {#custom-id}
</pre>

### Linking to Heading ID's
<pre>
[Heading IDs](#heading-ids)
</pre>

## Paragraph

<pre>
abcdefg
</pre>

## Line breaks
<pre>

## Emphasis

### Bold

<pre>
I **love** you.
I __love__ you.
</pre>

### Italic

<pre>
I *love* you.
I _love_ you.
</pre>


### Bold and Italic

<pre>
I ***love*** you.
I ___love___ you.
</pre>

### Strikethrough
<pre>
~~The world is flat.~~ We now know that the world is round.
</pre>

## Blockquotes

### Basic
<pre>
> I love you
</pre>

### With Multiple Paragraphs

<pre>
> I love you
>
> But I live in another city.
</pre>

### Nested

<pre>
> Can i help you?
>
>> No thanks.
</pre>

### With Another Elements

<pre>

> ## Can i help you?
>
> No **thanks**.
</pre>
