# Link

## Links

<pre>
I love [google](https://google.com).
</pre>

### Adding Titles
<pre>
I love [google](https://google.com "go to google").
</pre>

### Urls and Email
<pre>
<https://google.com>
<yseiren87@gmail.com>
</pre>

### With Format

<pre>
I love **[google](https://google.com)**.
I love *[google](https://google.com)*.
I love [code](#code).
</pre>

### Automatic URL Linkings
<pre>
http://www.example.com
</pre>

### Disabling Automatic URL Linking
<pre>
`http://www.example.com`
</pre>
